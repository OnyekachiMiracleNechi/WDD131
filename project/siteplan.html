Transport Company Website Plan

Site Name

TransportHub
This name was selected because it conveys a central location for all transport-related services and information.

Optional domain availability: transporthub.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as a comprehensive platform for transport services, offering information on logistics, transportation options, booking services, and customer support.

Scenarios

  • What transportation options are available for my area?
  • How can I track my shipment?
  • What are the rates for different transport services?
  • How do I get in touch with customer support?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #001f3f (navy blue for headings and buttons)
Secondary Color: #7fdbff (light blue for accents and highlights)
Accent Color: #ffcc00 (yellow for special elements)
Background Color: #ffffff (white for the main content area)

Typography

Heading Font: Arial Black (for main headings)
Body Font: Arial (for all body text)
Accent Font: Georgia (for quotes or special sections)
